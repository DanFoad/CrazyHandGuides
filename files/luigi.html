Luigi

Author: /u/gameonion

Luigi is a powerhouse if you know how to use him. So, how do we want to begin?

Basics - What you need to know!
First of all: Luigi is a slow character. His jump? Slow. His dash? Slow. Movement? Sluggish. So you need to know how to handle him.

  • Never jump too high, unless you are attacking.
  • Never stop running. Shield or Roll out of a run.
  • Never land on a platform with his Up-B.

Simple, right?

Recovery - How do?
His recovery his weird. Yes, he jumps high, but his air-movement is slow. Very, very slow. So how do you recover?

  • If you are far away from land, use Side-B.
  • If you are near enough, use Up-B.
  • Always save your jumps.

You can also use his Down-B as a recovery-move, but it's really hard and I don't recommend it. But if you want to use it... Press Down-B. After that, mash B as hard as you can.

"Combos" - I know they don't really exsist...
Luigi has some devestating "combo"s up his sleeves! I will list some now. I will tell you if they are easy, medium or hard difficulty.

  • D-Throw -> U-Tilt (0-20%) (Easy)
  • D-Throw -> N-Air -> F-Air / U-Air / D-Air (Any) (Easy)
  • D-Throw -> U-Special (50%) (Hard)
  • D-Throw -> D-Special -> Mash B (Any) (Easy)
  • Up-Smash -> Down B -> Down B -> Juggle (50%) (Medium)

These are some examples. You need to know that they don't always work. It depends on the situation.

Some strategies / useful things / facts.

  • Luigi's Side B has a chance to misfire. At around 40-50%, it probably is an instant K.O.! You will also fly a larger distance.
  • Luigi's down taunt can spike enemies that are hanging on to a ledge. You need to practise this. It's very hard to hit.
  • Up-B can kill at 50 % if you hit just right.
  • His down-B is one of the most useful attacks. Enemy trying to approach while you are in the air? Down-B into him. Enemy trying to spike you? Down B and mash B. It can also block some projectiles.
  • His B-Air is a really good kill move.
  • D-Air can Spike.
  • When your enemy is waiting for you near the ledge: Get near the ledge and you enemy, then use his U-Air. The hitbox is pretty big.
  • If your enemy is on the ground trying to stand up, dash into him. It will hit him.
  • The last frame of his dash-attack has knockback.
  • Stuck in enemy's combo / jab? Mash A. Has N-Air can often save you from combos. His Down-Special also kinda save you, if you pull it of just right.
